About me

Hi! My name is Claudio and welcome to my projects page. A bit about me, I am portuguese South African, born in SA and portuguese by descent. Grew up in a small mining town before heading to Pretoria to study Biotechnology at the University of Pretoria. After varsity, I spent several years working in the bio and chemistry space focussed on sales before moving to the UK. I am currently a Sales manager for a scientific equipment company, but I am interested in switching fields into the tech space. Hence I decided to start looking into web development and joined the front-end web development bootcamp.

More on the fun side, I enjoy gaming (I am a big fan of role playing games), spending time with my friends and girlfriend and travelling.

Looking forward to learning new things and will be updating my projects on this site as I go along.
